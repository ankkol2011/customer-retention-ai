- Monitoring pipeline: track data quality, prediction distribution, and review queue size.
- Drift detection pipeline: compare new customer data against the training baseline.

- Real-time API.
- Fully automated retraining.
- Advanced text modeling for `customer_feedback`.

Target MLOps maturity:
- Start at Level 1: reproducible ZenML pipelines, validation, versioned models.

- Avoid Level 3 full automation until monitoring proves stability.

Key production rule:
